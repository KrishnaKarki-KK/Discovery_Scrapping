# Everest Base Camp Trek Map
by [Paul Gurung](https://www.discoveryworldtrekking.com/blog/author/Paul)
ON 4 Jan, 2025
![Himalayan Image](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/4662/oooee.webp)
The Everest Base Camp trek is an iconic and classic journey that captivates the hearts of trekkers and adventure enthusiasts worldwide. This 14-day trek immerses you in the breathtaking beauty of the Khumbu region, allowing you to witness the majestic Himalayan peaks, experience the rich Sherpa culture, and culminate at the renowned Everest Base Camp.
The Everest Base Camp trekking is a transformative adventure through picturesque landscapes, charming Sherpa villages, and ancient monasteries. As you ascend to higher altitudes, each step is a testament to your perseverance, rewarded by panoramic views of some of the world's highest peaks. This article presents the Everest Base Camp trek map of this classical Everest Trek.

## **14-day Everest Base Camp Trek Map Itinerary**
The 14-day Everest Base Camp trek is the classic trek to Everest, covering around 80 miles or 130 kilometers. Here is a brief outline of the classic map of Everest Base Camp itinerary.
**Day 1** |  **Flight from Kathmandu (1,400m / 4,593ft) to Lukla (2,850m / 9,350ft). Trek to Phakding ( 2,650m / 8,562ft ).**
  * Thrilling 40-minute flight to Lukla
  * Trek from Lukla to Phakding

  
---|---  
**Day 2** |  **Trek from Phakding (2,650m / 8,562ft ) to Namche Bazaar (3,440m / 11,285ft)**
  * Cross the Dudh Koshi River on thrilling suspension bridges
  * Enter Sagarmatha National Park
  * First views of Mt. Everest

  
**Day 3** |  **Rest day and acclimatization at Namche Bazaar**
  * Side trips to Hillary School, Syangboche Airport
  * Hike to Everest View Hotel to see Everest

  
**Day 4** |  **Trek from Namche (3,440m / 11,285ft) to Tengboche/ Deboche (3,855m / 12,850ft)**
  * stunning views of Everest, Nuptse, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam
  * Phunki Thenga Village
  * Tengboche Monastery

  
**Day 5** |  **Trek from Tengboche (3,855m / 12,850ft) to Dingboche (4,360m / 14,290ft)**
  * Chortens, mani walls, and several small villages, including Pangboche
  * Fields surrounded by stone walls to protect crops

  
**Day 6** |  **Rest day and acclimatization at Dingboche**
  * spectacular views of mountains, Everest, Lhotse, Ama Dablam, Island Peak, Cho Oyu, & Makalu.
  * Optional hike to Chhukung for grander views

  
**Day 7** |  **Trek from Dingboche (4,360m / 14,290ft) to Lobuche (4,930m / 16,175ft)**
  * Trek alongside Khumbu Glacier
  * Everest Memorial at Thukla

  
**Day 8** |  **Trek from Lobuche (4,930m / 16,175ft) to EBC (5,364m / 17,598ft) and back to Gorak Shep (5,185m / 17,010ft)**
  * Leave your belongings in Gorak Shep
  * Step on Everest Base Camp enjoy the views and reflect on the struggle to reach EBC
  * Return back to Gorak Shep for the night

  
**Day 9** |  **Hike to Kala Patthar (5,555m / 18,208ft) viewpoint, trek to Gorak Shep then to Pheriche (4,250m/13,945ft)**
  * Early morning hike to Kala Patthar viewpoint for grand views of Everest

  
**Day 10** |  **Trek from Pheriche (4,250m / 13,945ft) to Tengboche (3,855m / 12,850ft)**
  * Descend through Rhododendron and Juniper forests
  * Cross Imja Khola

  
**Day 11** |  **Trek from Tengboche (3,855m / 12,850ft) to Namche Bazaar (3,440m / 11,285ft)**
  * Chance to observe pheasants, musk deer, mountain goats, and even snow leopards.
  * See numerous prayer flags, chortens, the backside of Lhotse, and Mt. Everest. 

  
**Day 12** |  **Trek from Namche Bazaar (3,440m / 11,285ft) to Phakding (2,650m / 8,690ft)**
  * Follow the banks of the Dudh Koshi River 
  * Cross many suspension bridges

  
**Day 13** |  **Trek from Phakding (2,650m / 8,690ft) to Lukla (2,850m / 9,350ft)**
  * Very short trek
  * Pass by Toktok village
  * Different views of the Himalayas

  
**Day 14** |  **Fly back to Kathmandu (1400m / 4,593ft) from Lukla (2,850m / 9,350ft)**
  * A thrilling 40-minute flight from Lukla to Kathmandu
  * Farewell dinner in Kathmandu

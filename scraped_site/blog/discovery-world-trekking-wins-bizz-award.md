# Discovery World Trekking Wins Bizz Award for Business Excellence
by [Paul Gurung](https://www.discoveryworldtrekking.com/blog/author/Paul)
ON 24 Jul, 2025
![Himalayan Image](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/5174/dio.webp)
We feel honored to receive the 2024 Business Excellence Certificate from the World Confederation of Businesses (WORLDCOB). WORLDCOB is an American Company based in Houston, Texas, with an aim to recognize and boost the leading businesses worldwide.
We are a leading Nepali trek and tour company with over a decade of experience in the tourism industry. Since our inception, we have focused on providing a rich trek experience as our guests enjoy the incredible Nepali terrain, which ranges from the deepest gorge (Kali Gandaki Gorge) to the highest mountain peak (Mt. Everest). 
This award is precious to us as it recognizes our effort to provide quality service to our guests who want to experience Nepal.  
  

## **Award Details**
The Bizz Awards recognize leading businesses from all continents that significantly contribute to their local and world economy. The award takes into account business leadership, management systems, quality of goods and services, innovation, social responsibility, and achievements. 
[WORLDCOB](https://worldcob.org/) contacted Discovery World Trekking, perhaps due to our reputation in Nepal and abroad.
The Bizz Award acknowledges our company’s excellence and leadership in the trek and tour industry. This award will further enhance our reputation and strengthen our position globally. 
We hope our potential clients will know about us and our service quality due to this award. It will also help recognize Nepal as a tourist hub. 
![](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/5177/featured-image-min.webp)
## **Company Achievements**
Discovery World Trekking’s dedication to maintaining high standards is evident in the quality of service we provide to our clients. Our Clients have consistently praised the company for its experienced guides, well-organized trips, and unparalleled service. 
  * ### Excellence in Customer Service


Discovery World Trekking has built a reputation for delivering exceptional customer service. The company’s personalized approach, attention to detail, and commitment to client satisfaction distinguish it in the competitive adventure tourism market. Clients consistently praise the professionalism and dedication of the company’s guides and staff, who go above and beyond to ensure a safe and memorable trekking experience.
At the time of writing this article, we have received 5 (out of 5) ratings in Google from 577 [Google reviews](https://g.co/kgs/XrFfe61), 5 (out of 5) ratings in [Tripadvisor](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g293890-d6592958-Reviews-Discovery_World_Trekking-Kathmandu_Kathmandu_Valley_Bagmati_Zone_Central_Region.html) (1,261 reviews), and 4.9 (out of 5) rating at [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/discoveryworldtrekking) (365 reviews).
  * ### Innovative Trekking Packages


Our company offers popular trekking packages (like [Everest Base Camp Trek](https://www.discoveryworldtrekking.com/trips/everest-base-camp-trek)) and custom treks that cater to various skill levels and preferences, ensuring an unforgettable adventure. Our personalized approach, heart-warming hospitality, care, and trek expertise sets us apart in the industry.
  * ### Sustainable and Responsible Tourism


As a responsible company, we are committed to sustainable tourism, implementing eco-friendly practices, and supporting local communities in the trekking regions. Our policy is to leave only the footprints in the trek regions to preserve the ecology. We also associate with the Kathmandu Environmental Education Project (KEEP) to ensure our treks are environmentally friendly. 
![](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/5178/14.webp)
## **Leadership and DWT Family**
[Paul Gurung](https://paulgurung.com/), CEO and Founder of Discovery World Trekking says, "We are immensely proud to receive the 2024 Business Excellence Certificate from WORLDCOB. This award recognizes our hard work and dedication to providing exceptional trekking experiences while promoting sustainable tourism. 
He modestly adds, “This award was possible not only because of me but because of everyone in our DWT family.” 
Along with Discovery World Trekking, the WORLDCOB has awarded Paul Gurung a Business Excellence Certificate for being a successful leader.
**We express our sincere gratitude to the entire Discovery Family for this achievement**. 
![](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/5179/hawi.webp)

Discovery World Trekking aims to broaden its horizons and reach new heights in the adventure tourism industry. The company plans to enhance its current operations, offering clients a wider range of unique and exciting experiences within the Himalayan region.

# Top 5 Must Go Trekking places in Nepal
by [Paul Gurung](https://www.discoveryworldtrekking.com/blog/author/Paul)
ON 24 Jul, 2025
![Himalayan Image](https://cms.discoveryworldtrekking.com/media/4092/VS-\(2\).webp)
Nepal is a South Asian country located between China and India, a place where Lord Gautam Buddha was born, a country that has the **highest peaks in the world,** and many other titles that come from the rich history of Nepal. While this Himalayan nation is famous for so many things, Nepal is very famous for outdoor activities like trekking, hiking, mountain climbing, and lists just do end. Thrilling Bungee jumping to rafting in amazing fast current rivers, Nepal is a country that has everything when it comes to outdoor activities. Today, if you search in Google, you’ll find 100s of articles that have top destinations to visit in Nepal but today we have come up with the list of the most amazing and beautiful trekking destinations where you’ll have once in a lifetime experience for sure. **Trekking in Nepal** is amazing everywhere but out of all these 5 are all-time favorites among trekkers.  
  

##  1. Everest Base Camp  

  
**Everest Base Camp** is one of the must-visit places in the world. **Gateway of Mighty Everest** , people from all over the world recognize this place as classic trekking trails in Sherpa Village. This journey begins from Kathmandu (The city of temples). EBC can be one of the best places to visit as you’ll experience the unique adventure in the Himalayan nation. This journey consists of World’s highest airport in Syangboche, the Highest glacier on Earth- Khumbu Glacier (4,900 m), Ancient monasteries, and Nepal’s 1st Natural Heritage site (Sagarmatha National Park). This journey is truly a once in a lifetime experience as people get to explore so many things before reaching the final destination. Other notable highlights of the EBC trek are Kalapathar, Mountain flight from Kathmandu to Lukla, Gokyo lake, and many more. No wonder why Everest Base Camp is one of the world’s best trekking trails.
##  2. Annapurna base camp  

  
Annapurna Base Camp is the hottest destination when it comes to trekking in Nepal. **Giant travel** sites like Lonely Planet, Tripadvisor, TourRadar, and many others have officially promoted ABC as once in a lifetime experience destination. ABC is relatively easier to trek compared to other trails which make this place even more accessible to many people. The landscape, view, and majestic mountains are just feasted eyes and peace to the soul. the fantastic sight of glaciers, and mountain range from Hiunchuli, past Annapurna South to **Annapurna I (8,097m/26,566ft)** Gang**apurna, Khangsar Kang, Annapurna III** , and Machhapuchhare. This journey is full of magical views, temples, monasteries and the best part is the journey begins and ends in Pokhara (A beautiful valley in Nepal). A **package of Lake, more than 5 snow-capped mountains, Annapurna Base camp** itself is really worth every penny and comes with an amazing peace experience. 
## 3. Manaslu circuit trek  

**Mt Manaslu** is the 8th Highest Mountain in the world with an elevation of 8,163 m. It’s one of the most popular expedition destinations among Japanese as it was 1st conquered by the team of Toshio Imanishi and Gyalzen Norbu from Japan on May 9, 1956. If you want to conquer a mountain that is known as the “mountain of the spirit” you should definitely put it in bucket list or alternatively Manaslu circuit trek is ultimately off beaten hiking trails in Nepal. The untouched off beaten paths with the mesmerizing landscape, **Manaslu Circuit trek** truly a gift of nature. With an amazing viewpoint for Ganesh Himal (7,422m) and Mt. Himalchuli (7,893m) trek itself is the ultimate package of fun and memories. Some of the notable places in the Manaslu Circuit trek journey are astonishing views of **Larkya Glacier and the Larkya peak** followed by trekking around Manaslu crossing the Larkya La Pass (5,100m) eventually reaching Bhimthang then to Gho. With these many viewpoints and amazing experience of off beaten trails, Manaslu Circuit Trek is really something to do when traveling Nepal.  

## 4. Langtang Valley Trek  

**Mt Langtang** is yet another one of the most visited destinations in Nepal. Just 51km north of Kathmandu, Langtang Valley Trek has a treasure of natural gifts. It’s home to Langtang National park, Kyanjin Ri, and a Homeland of snow-capped mountains, waterfalls, and several suspension bridges. People from all over the world prefer this nearest destination from the Capital of the country. The journey Consists of several **Majestic viewpoints of Langtang Lirung (7,345m), Gang Chhenpo (6,388m), Naya Kanga (5,846m), DorjeLakpa (6,966m), Kimsung (6,781m), and YansaTsenji (6,575m).** Every day of the trek is Viewpoint day as all you see is 360 panoramic view of breathtaking snow-capped mountains. As the trek is relatively easier, almost any age group can join this venture. A journey where you’ll encounter the local culture of Native people who live in Langtang National park can really be a once in a lifetime experience. If you're traveling to Nepal anytime soon, this 8 days trek to Langtang Valley can really be a unique experience. No wonder why this destination is **one of the most visited.**  
  

##  5. Ruby Valley trek (Ganesh Himal Trek)  

Ruby Valley trek is a deal-breaker for trek enthusiasts all over the world as it's a very new destination which was open for tourism just a few years ago. If you’re one of the people who love nature **Discovery and go to unexplored places** , Ruby Valley Trek can be a perfect destination in Nepal. An unexplored beauty amidst the Himalayan Nation, Ruby Valley Trek is a package of scenic elegance and cultural and traditional virtue. It’s one of the easiest trails with so much to offer. It’s home to dense forests of Rhododendron, pine, oak, and bamboo with Several rivers along with suspension bridges, Georges, waterfalls, and stone- driven mills. You’ll also encounter Ruby, Crystal, Zinc mines in the mountains which can be a unique experience for most of us. **Ruby Valley** is also known for the beauty of the trail and is intensified with a glimpse of the amazing Annapurna range, Langtang range, Ganesh Himal range, and Manaslu range followed by the highest point of the journey: exquisite Pangsang La Pass. If you’re an explorer and want to explore something different in just 7 days, putting Ruby Valley Trek on your bucket list might be a smart move while **traveling in Nepal.**
